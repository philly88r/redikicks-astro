<h2>Application Techniques</h2>
<p>Proper application techniques are crucial for maximizing the efficacy of night creams and ensuring optimal absorption of active ingredients into the skin.</p>
<p>Begin by thoroughly cleansing the face to remove impurities and excess oils.</p>
<p>Apply the night cream to slightly damp skin&#44; which enhances absorption.</p>
<p>Use gentle&#44; upward strokes to massage a pea-sized amount of product into the face and neck&#44; avoiding the delicate eye area unless the cream is specifically formulated for that purpose.</p>
<p>For men&#44; application after shaving or on light stubble is recommended.</p>
<p>Consistency is key&#59; incorporate the night cream into your nightly routine for best results.</p>
<p>Allow sufficient time between application and sleep to prevent product transfer to pillowcases.</p>
<p>Remember&#44; a little goes a long way&#59; over-application can lead to clogged pores and reduced effectiveness.</p>
<p>Adjust the amount based on your skin&#39;s needs and the product&#39;s consistency.</p>
