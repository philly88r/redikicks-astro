<p>I&#39;ve learned that a few essential balancing techniques can help me stand confidently&#58;</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Foot Placement</strong>&#58; I should position my feet shoulder-width apart&#44; with my back foot near the tail. This helps me with stability and control.</li>
<li><strong>Weight Distribution</strong>&#58; I must keep my weight centered over the board&#44; leaning slightly forward. If I lean too far back&#44; I risk losing balance and wiping out.</li>
<li><strong>Engaging Core Muscles</strong>&#58; Activating my core is crucial. It helps me stay balanced and shifts my weight smoothly&#44; allowing for quick adjustments.</li>
</ol>
