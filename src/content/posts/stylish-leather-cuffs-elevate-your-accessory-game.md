<p>Creating high-quality leather cuffs means focusing on durability. Using top-notch materials makes sure your accessory lasts long and looks great. A mix of different leathers and strong hardware makes these cuffs both useful and stylish.</p>
<h3>Materials Used in Leather Cuffs</h3>
<p>Strong materials are key for making leather cuffs last. Brands often pick <em>bison leather</em> for its tight grain and big waves, giving it a nice look. <em>Bulldog leather</em> is another top choice, known for its tough appearance from a special shrinking process.</p>
<p><em>Grizzly leather</em> has a soft touch and a natural look, thanks to a veggie-tanned finish. <em>Ranch leather</em> gets a shiny finish through an Italian process. </p>
