<h3>How do I choose the right hair styling product for my hair type?</h3>
<div>
<div>
<p>Picking the right product depends on your hair type and style. Consider if your hair is straight, wavy, or curly. Think about whether you want sleek or textured hair. This will help you choose between a pomade, clay, or cream.</p>
</div>
</div>

<h3>What is the difference between hair wax, pomade, and clay?</h3>
<div>
<div>
<p>Waxes, pomades, and clays offer different holds and textures. Waxes give a strong hold and shine. Pomades offer medium to high hold with various shine levels. Clays provide a matte, textured hold for messy, natural styles.</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<h3>How do I apply hair styling products effectively?</h3>
<div>
<div>
<p>Start with a small amount of product on damp or dry hair. Focus on styling areas you want. Use your fingers to spread the product and shape your style. Too much product can make your hair look greasy.</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div>
<h3>How often should I wash out hair styling products?</h3>
<div>
<div>
<p>Washing out styling products depends on the type and your hair. Water-based products like creams and gels wash out easily. Waxes and pomades may need more frequent shampooing. Wash out styling products at least a few times a week for clean, healthy hair.</p>
