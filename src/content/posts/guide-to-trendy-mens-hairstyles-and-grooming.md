<h3>Daily Care Essentials</h3>

<p>Here&#39;s what you need to keep in mind:</p>
<ol>
<li>Cleanse Gently: Use a sulfate-free shampoo suited for your hair type. This ensures your scalp stays healthy without stripping away natural oils.</li>
<li>Condition Wisely: Don&#39;t skip conditioner! It hydrates and detangles your hair, making styling easier. Opt for a lightweight formula if you have fine hair or a richer one for thick locks.</li>
<li>Style Smart: Be mindful of how you style your hair. Use heat protectants when blow-drying or straightening to prevent damage.</li>
</ol>
<p>Regularly switch up your styles to avoid stress on specific areas of your scalp.</p>
