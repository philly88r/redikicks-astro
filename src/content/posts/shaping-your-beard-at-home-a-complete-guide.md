<h2>Preparing Your Beard for Shaping</h2>
<p>Before you start shaping your beard, you need to get it ready. First, wash your beard with a special <em>beard shampoo</em>. This removes dirt and old products. Then, gently pat it dry without rubbing too hard.</p>
<p>Next, put a bit of <em>beard oil</em> on your beard and skin. It makes your whiskers soft and easy to shape. Beard oil also keeps your beard healthy and looking good.</p>
<h3>Washing and Drying Your Beard</h3>
<p>Begin by washing your beard with <em>beard shampoo</em>. Massage it into your beard and skin well. Then, rinse with warm water to get rid of any soap left.</p>
<p>Pat your beard dry with a towel. Be gentle so you don&#8217;t mess up the hair&#8217;s growth.</p>
<h3>Applying Beard Oil for Manageability</h3>
<p>After drying, put a few drops of <em>beard oil</em> on your palms. Rub it into your beard and skin. This makes your beard soft and easy to shape. It also keeps your skin from getting itchy or flaky.</p>
