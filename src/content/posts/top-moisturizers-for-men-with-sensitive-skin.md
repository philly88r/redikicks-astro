<h2>Application Tips for Sensitive Skin</h2>
<p>Caring for sensitive skin means more than just picking the right moisturizer. How you apply it is just as crucial. Here are some easy tips to keep your skin healthy and moist:</p>
<h3>Patch Testing for Potential Irritants</h3>
<p>Always do a patch test before using a new product. Put a small amount on your inner elbow. Wait 24-48 hours to see if it causes any problems. This step helps find out if the product will irritate your skin.</p>
<h3>Proper Cleansing and Gentle Exfoliation</h3>
<p>Cleaning and exfoliating are important steps before moisturizing. Use a gentle cleanser that doesn&#8217;t dry out your skin. Exfoliate 1-2 times a week with a soft cloth or a mild chemical exfoliant. This removes dead skin without hurting your skin.</p>
<p>By following these tips, you can make your moisturizer work better. Enjoy healthy, moist skin all day.</p>
