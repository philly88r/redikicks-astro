<h2>Hair Maintenance Best Practices</h2>

<p>Maintaining your medium length hairstyle requires consistent trims and the right hair care practices to keep your locks healthy and stylish.</p>
<p>Schedule regular trims every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and shape your cut.</p>
<p>Your hair products play a vital role&#59; opt for high-quality shampoos and conditioners specifically tailored for your hair type.</p>
