<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><em>Slip-resistant soles</em> for a strong grip on the pedals</li>



<li><em>Flexible uppers</em> that move with the foot&#8217;s natural motion</li>



<li><em>Lightweight construction</em> to reduce foot weight</li>



<li><em>Low-profile design</em> to not block pedal movement</li>
</ul>



<p>Driving shoes are known for their small rubber pebbles or nubs on the sole. These add extra grip and stop heel-wear that regular shoes can cause. This special design is key for giving precise control and feedback while driving.</p>
