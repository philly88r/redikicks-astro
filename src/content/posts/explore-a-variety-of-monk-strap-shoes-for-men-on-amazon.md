<p>Monk strap shoes have a rich history, dating back to the Middle Ages. European monks wore them. These shoes have a <a href="https://redikicks.com/?p=52" title="Browse a variety of men’s slip-on shoes available on Amazon.">wide strap</a> across the front, held by one or two buckles. <h3 class="wp-block-heading">History and Origins of the Monkstrap Style</h3>



<p>The <a href="https://www.beckettsimonon.com/blogs/resources/what-is-a-monkstrap-shoe-your-ultimate-guide" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">history of monk strap shoes</a> started in the late 19th century. Edward Green, an English shoemaker, created this style. It&#8217;s considered semi-formal, between an Oxford and a Derby shoe. Monk strap shoes became a popular choice for their unique look.</p>
