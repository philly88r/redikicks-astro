<h2>The Perfect Placement for Your Tie Clip</h2>
<p>Knowing where to put your tie clip is key to looking sharp and balanced. It should sit between the third and fourth buttons of your shirt. This spot makes your outfit look even and keeps the tie clip doing its job.</p>
<p>Putting the clip wrong can mess up your look, making it less elegant. So, make sure it&#8217;s in the right spot.</p>
