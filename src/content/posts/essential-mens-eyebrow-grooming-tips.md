<h2>Finding Your Eyebrow Start and End Points</h2>
<p>Proper eyebrow grooming starts with finding the right shape. To get symmetrical and well-defined eyebrows, you must know where they should start and end.</p>
<p>First, find where your eyebrows should start. Hold a fine-tooth comb vertically against your nostril&#8217;s side. This shows where your eyebrows should begin. Use high-quality tweezers to remove stray hairs in the middle. Leave a few small hairs for a natural look.</p>
<p>Next, find where your eyebrows should end. Use the comb to draw an imaginary line from your nose tip to your eye&#8217;s outer corner. Remove any hairs outside this line to get a clean, symmetrical brow shape.</p>
<ol>
<li>Locate the starting point of your eyebrows by holding a comb vertically against the side of your nostril.</li>
<li>Pluck any stray hairs in the middle, leaving a few small hairs for a natural look.</li>
<li>Determine the endpoint of your eyebrows by drawing an imaginary line from the tip of your nose to the outer corner of your eye.</li>
<li>Remove any excess hairs outside this line to achieve a clean, symmetrical brow shape.</li>
</ol>
<p>By following these steps, you can <em>locate the start and end points of your eyebrows</em>. This ensures proper <em>eyebrow placement</em> and <em>symmetrical brows</em>. A well-groomed and balanced brow can make you look and feel better.</p>

<h2>Trimming vs. Tweezing from the Top</h2>
<p>After tweezing the start and end of your eyebrows, it&#8217;s time for a finer touch. But, when using <a href="https://redikicks.com/top-eyebrow-trimmers-for-the-modern-man/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">proper eyebrow trimming techniques</a>, always tweeze from the bottom. Tweezing from the top can make your brows too curved, which most men don&#8217;t want. Keeping the top hairs in check helps your brows look full and young.</p>
<p>If your eyebrows are too long, use a fine-tooth comb to lift the hairs. Then, use precision-tip scissors to trim the visible hairs. This method <em>avoids over-arched brows</em> and <em>keeps your brows full</em>, making your look natural and neat.</p>

<h2>Leave Some Gray Hairs for a Natural Look</h2>
<p>It&#8217;s tempting to remove every gray hair from your eyebrows.
