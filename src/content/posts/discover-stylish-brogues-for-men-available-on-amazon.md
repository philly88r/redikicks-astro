<h2>What are Brogues?</h2>
<h3>Historical Origins of Brogues</h3>
<p>Brogues have a long history, starting in the late 1700s and early 1800s. They were first made for Irish and Scottish workers who needed shoes that let water out. This was because they worked in wet places like bogs and marshes.</p>
<p>These shoes changed from being just for work to being fashionable. The holes in the shoes were once for draining water but now make the shoes look unique. Famous people like Fred Astaire and Gene Kelly wore them, making them popular in <b>men&#8217;s fashion</b>.</p>
