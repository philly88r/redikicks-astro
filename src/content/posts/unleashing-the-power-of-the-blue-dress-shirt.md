<h2>Care and Maintenance Tips</h2>
<p>Caring for your blue dress shirt not only preserves its pristine look but also extends its lifespan&#44; making it a staple in your wardrobe for years to come.</p>
<p>Start by following the care label instructions&#44; opting for cold wash techniques to maintain color vibrancy.</p>
