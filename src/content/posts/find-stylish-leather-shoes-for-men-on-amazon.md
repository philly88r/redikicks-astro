<h2>Caring for Your Leather Shoes</h2>
<p>Proper care and maintenance are key to keeping your <a href="https://thehelmclothing.com/blogs/editorial/how-to-care-for-leather-shoes" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">leather shoes</a> looking great. <h3>Cleaning and Conditioning</h3>
<p>Clean your leather shoes right after wearing them to stop dirt from building up. Use a mild leather cleaner and a soft cloth for the job. Also, condition your leather every few months to keep it soft and prevent cracks.</p>
<h3>Protecting Against Water and Stains</h3>
<p>It&#8217;s important to keep your leather shoes safe from water and stains. Use a waterproofing spray to protect your shoes. This keeps them looking good for a long time. But, don&#8217;t use silicone-based sprays because they can harm the leather.</p>
