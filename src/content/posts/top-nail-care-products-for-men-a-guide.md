<h3>Application Tips for Men</h3>
<p>Mastering the art of nail polish application can transform your grooming routine and enhance your overall look. If you want to elevate your style, follow these essential nail health tips and improve your nail grooming habits:</p>
<ol>
<li>Prep Your Nails: Start with clean, dry nails. Remove any old polish and ensure your nails are free of oils and dirt. This step is crucial for a smooth application.</li>
<li>Use a Base Coat: Applying a base coat protects your nails and helps the polish adhere better. Don&#39;t skip this step; it&#39;ll prolong the life of your manicure.</li>
<li>Apply in Thin Layers: Instead of slapping on thick coats, go for thin layers. This prevents bubbling and ensures even coverage. Wait a few minutes between coats for the best results.</li>
<li>Finish with a Top Coat: Seal your masterpiece with a top coat for added shine and durability. It&#39;ll help prevent chipping and keep your nails looking fresh longer.</li>
</ol>
