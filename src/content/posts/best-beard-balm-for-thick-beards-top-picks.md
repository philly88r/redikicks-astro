<h2>Beard Balm Application Techniques</h2>
<p>Getting the most out of your <strong>beard balm</strong> starts with how you apply it. First, make sure your beard is clean and a bit damp. Then, take a small amount of balm and warm it in your palms. Use your fingers to spread it evenly, from your skin to the ends of your hair.</p>
<p>After applying, use a <a href="https://www.reuzel.com/blogs/news/how-to-use-beard-balm?srsltid=AfmBOopm60Ij0GwldaHy-Hc6rklkb-PoEQdyD-PdAuZmU9IGZISasbOA" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">beard brush or comb</a> to style. This makes sure the balm is spread out right and keeps your beard looking neat.</p>
<p>Use beard balm every time after washing and when your beard itches. It&#8217;s best to apply after a shower when your beard is cleanest. If you use both beard oil and balm, apply oil in the morning and balm at night.</p>
<ol>
<li>Prep your beard and skin by cleansing and gently drying.</li>
<li>Scoop out a small amount of beard balm and warm it between your palms.</li>
<li>Work the balm through your beard, starting from the skin and moving towards the ends.</li>
<li>Use a <b>beard brush</b> or comb to style and evenly distribute the balm.</li>
</ol>
