<h2>How to Properly Care for No-Show Socks</h2>
<p>How long your no-show socks last depends on how you take care of them. Make sure to wash them in cold water to keep them stretchy. This stops high heat from damaging them. Following these <em>laundry tips</em> keeps your socks looking good and working right.</p>
<p>Choose gentle detergents to wash them with. This keeps them clean and the material strong. Dry them on a line or use a low heat setting. This keeps the fabric in top shape. Washing them often also keeps the silicone grips working well, so they don&#8217;t slip down.</p>
