<h2>What is a Trench Coat?</h2>
<p>The trench coat is a classic piece of clothing with a military background. It was first made to keep soldiers warm and dry in World War I trenches. Now, it&#8217;s a must-have for both men and women in the fashion world.</p>
<h3>Origins and History of the Trench Coat</h3>
<p>The trench coat started in the early 1900s. Brands like <em>Burberry</em> and <em>Aquascutum</em> created it. They used special <b>water-resistant</b> fabric and added features like belts and epaulets.</p>
<p>These features helped soldiers stay safe in the trenches. After the war, the trench coat became popular among civilians. Stars like <em>Audrey Hepburn</em> and <em>Marilyn Monroe</em> wore them, making the trench coat a fashion icon.</p>
