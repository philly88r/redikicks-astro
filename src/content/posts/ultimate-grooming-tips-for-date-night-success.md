<h3>Polish Application Techniques</h3>
<p>Getting your nail polish application just right can make all the difference in how your manicure looks and lasts. Nail polish techniques are key to achieving that perfect finish, so let&#39;s dive into some effective color application tips.</p>
<ol>
<li>Brush Selection Guide: Choose the right brush for your polish. A wider brush works well for quick coverage, while a finer brush is great for intricate nail art ideas.</li>
<li>Drying Time Tricks: To speed up drying, use thin layers of polish. Thick layers take forever to dry and can smudge easily.</li>
<li>Top Coat Importance: Always finish with a quality top coat. It not only enhances shine but also protects your manicure from chips, extending its life.</li>
</ol>
<p>When applying polish, consider layering methods for a unique look. You can mix colors or add a glitter top coat for some fun finish options.</p>
