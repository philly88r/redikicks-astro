<h2>Care and Maintenance Tips</h2>
<p>Keeping your <b>military-inspired outerwear</b> and <b>thermal outdoor apparel</b> in top shape is crucial. It ensures they stay warm and stylish all season long. Whether it&#8217;s a wool coat or an insulated work jacket, always follow the care instructions from the manufacturer.</p>
<p>Wool coats need special care. They take 2-3 days to dry, so flip them often. Wash them in lukewarm water, either by hand or in a machine. Never use a tumble dryer, as it can shrink the wool. For stains, spot clean and gently brush the fabric before washing.</p>
<p>Insulated work coats also need regular care. Wash them every 2-3 weeks and spot clean with a soft brush or damp cloth. Avoid dry cleaning, as it can harm the insulation. If you must machine wash, use a mild detergent and warm water. Then, air dry for up to 10 minutes before finishing with air drying.</p>
<p>By following these <em>care and maintenance</em> tips for your <em>military-inspired outerwear</em> and <em>thermal outdoor apparel</em>, your winter gear will stay reliable and stylish all season.</p>
