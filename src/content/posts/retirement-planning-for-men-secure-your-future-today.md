<h2>Creating a Sustainable Income Stream</h2>
<p>As you plan for retirement&#44; creating a <strong>sustainable income stream</strong> is crucial to maintaining your lifestyle.</p>

<h3>Investing in Dividends</h3>
<p>Many men nearing retirement can create a <strong>sustainable income stream</strong> by investing in <strong>dividend-paying stocks</strong>&#44; which have historically provided a relatively stable source of returns. By incorporating <strong>dividend growth strategies</strong> into your investment portfolio&#44; you can potentially increase your income over time. This approach involves investing in established companies with a history of consistently paying dividends and increasing their payouts.</p>
<p>When selecting dividend-paying stocks&#44; consider the following key factors&#58;</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Dividend yield</strong>&#58; The ratio of the annual dividend payment to the stock&#39;s current price.</li>
<li><strong>Payout ratio</strong>&#58; The percentage of earnings paid out as dividends.</li>
<li><strong>Dividend growth rate</strong>&#58; The rate at which the company has increased its dividend payments over time.</li>
<li>Financial health&#58; The company&#39;s ability to maintain its dividend payments.</li>
</ul>
