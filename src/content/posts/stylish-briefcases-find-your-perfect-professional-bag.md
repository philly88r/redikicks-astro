<h2>How to Care for Your Briefcase</h2>
<p>Keeping your briefcase in good shape is key, no matter its material. It&#8217;s important to clean it regularly. For leather briefcases, use a quality leather conditioner, $19.95, once a year or more if you live in a dry area. Always dry leather with a cloth and let it air dry. Don&#8217;t rub the leather to avoid damage.</p>
<h3>Cleaning and Maintenance Tips</h3>
<p>Using a leather cleaner, $14.95, will also keep your briefcase looking great. Clean the inside with a damp cloth every month to get rid of dust and dirt. Don&#8217;t store your leather briefcase in humid places, direct sunlight, or near heat. Keep it in a fabric bag or box to protect it from dust and damage.</p>
<h3>Storage Solutions for Longevity</h3>
<p>Store your briefcase upright to keep it from getting deformed. Don&#8217;t pack it too full to avoid damage. This way, your briefcase will stay in good shape and last longer. Taking good care of your briefcase means it will look great and work well for many years.</p>
