<h2>What are Oxford Shoes?</h2>
<p>Oxford shoes are a <b>classic style</b> of <a href="https://www.beckettsimonon.com/blogs/resources/what-are-oxfords" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">men&#8217;s dress shoes</a> that have been popular for many years. They are known for their sleek look, closed lacing, and low profile. Oxfords represent <b>classic style</b> and elegance.</p>
<h3>Defining the Classic Oxford Style</h3>
<p>The classic Oxford shoe has a smooth look with a thin leather sole. It has a minimalist design. It&#8217;s often seen with a closed lacing system. This means the vamp and quarters are sewn together for a clean look.</p>
<h3>Key Features of Oxford Shoes</h3>
<ul>
<li>Leather upper: Oxfords are made from quality <em>leather</em>. This makes them <b>durable</b> and looks refined.</li>
<li>Closed lacing system: The <em>Balmoral</em> style lacing is a key feature of Oxfords. It makes them look sleek and formal.</li>
<li>Low heel: Oxfords have a low heel, under an inch tall. This gives them a sleek, <b>elegant</b> look.</li>
<li>Minimalist design: Oxfords are simple and <b>elegant</b>. They don&#8217;t have many decorations. This lets the leather and <b>craftsmanship</b> stand out.</li>
</ul>
<p>Oxfords can be worn with a sharp suit for formal events or with chinos and a button-down shirt for a casual look. <strong>Oxford shoes</strong> are versatile and a must-have for any man&#8217;s shoe collection.</p>
