<h2>Care and Maintenance</h2>
<p>Proper care and maintenance of stacked jeans is vital to preserving their unique distressed appeal and ensuring longevity in your wardrobe. Follow these essential steps&#58;</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Washing Techniques</strong>&#58; Always turn your jeans inside out before washing them in cold water. This prevents fading and reduces wear on the distressing.</li>
<li><strong>Drying Strategy</strong>&#58; Avoid using a dryer&#59; instead&#44; air dry your jeans to maintain their shape and integrity. Heat can cause fabric shrinkage and distortion.</li>
<li><strong>Repair Tips</strong>&#58; Invest in a fabric patch or needle and thread for minor rips. Regularly check for loose threads or seams that may need fixing to prolong the life of your favorite jeans.</li>
</ol>
