<h2>Cashmere Care and Maintenance</h2>
<p>To preserve the luxurious feel and longevity of your cashmere investments&#44; proper care and maintenance are essential.</p>
<p>Cashmere demands gentle handling to retain its softness and shape. Hand washing or dry cleaning are recommended methods&#44; with emphasis on using lukewarm water and mild detergents specifically formulated for delicate fabrics. Avoid wringing or twisting the garment&#59; instead&#44; gently press out excess water and lay flat to dry away from direct heat or sunlight.</p>
<p>Regular maintenance includes storing cashmere pieces flat to maintain their form and checking for pilling&#8212;those small balls of fiber that can accumulate on the surface. Remove these carefully with a cashmere comb or fabric shaver to keep the material smooth.</p>
<p>When not in use&#44; protect your cashmere from moths by storing in breathable garment bags with cedar blocks.</p>
<p>With proper care&#44; your cashmere accessories will remain luxurious staples in your autumn&#47;winter wardrobe for years to come.</p>
