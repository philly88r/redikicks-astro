<h2>Caring for Your Jeans</h2>
<p>Proper care and maintenance of your jeans can significantly extend their lifespan&#44; ensuring that your investment in quality denim pays dividends for years to come.</p>
<p>To preserve your denim&#39;s integrity&#44; wash them sparingly&#8212;ideally every 4-6 months&#8212;using cold water and a gentle cycle. Turn jeans inside out before washing to minimize fading and protect the outer surface. Avoid using a dryer&#59; instead&#44; hang them to air dry&#44; preferably outdoors but away from direct sunlight.</p>
<p>For spot cleaning&#44; use a damp cloth with mild soap. To maintain shape between washes&#44; fold jeans neatly or hang them by the waistband. Address any repairs promptly to prevent further damage.</p>
<p>When storing&#44; keep jeans in a cool&#44; dry place to prevent mold growth. These practices will help retain your jeans&#39; fit&#44; color&#44; and overall quality.</p>
